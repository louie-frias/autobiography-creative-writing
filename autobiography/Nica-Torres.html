Nica Torres

Hi my name is Nica. I was born on November 24,2005 in a small village in the province of Bayambang. I grew up in a simple family with six members. My father is a farmer, while my mother is a house wife, we live in a simple house with a wide field of different crops around.

When I was young, our life was simple and full of fun and prudence. I along with my siblings, help plant the fields, this teaches us discipline and caring for our parents.

When I was a student at our school, I met teachers who contributed to my development. My love for books and learning started when I was still in Elementary School. My teachers inspired me to be a good student.

When I went to High School, I became more active in school works, and had friends who became important in my life. Our travels and happy memories give life to my everyday life.

And some years passed I am now a Senior High School student in a Private School called SVCSBI, I am a person towards my dream not even smart but have a good life and just simple I am not as smart as others but I continue just dreaming.

My dreams for my future are to have a more successful career in the technology field and to have a deeper connection with my family. I want to help my parents and siblings and inspire others who dream and work hard like me.

Despite the trials and successes in my life, I learned that determination and hard work are important in achieving dreams. My journey continues, and I am ready to face the challenges that will come in my life.

In my eyes, life is abundant even if we live simply. Being happy and content with what we have is the most important thing. My autobiography is proof that a simple life can be full of value and happiness.
