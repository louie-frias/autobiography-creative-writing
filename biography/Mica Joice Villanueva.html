Mica Joice Villanueva

Ma. Jasmin Niña S. Pingul, 18 years of age, born on January 17, 2006 at Cebu City, Cebu. She is a part of the Roman Catholic community. She currently lives at Brgy. Alinggan, Bayambang, Pangasinan. She has multiple nicknames like Jas, Ja, or Jaja. Jasmin is the daughter of Menche B. Seguin, an OFW, and John D. Pingul, a Seaman. She has 2 younger siblings. She is ambitious but, most of the times like to overthink a lot of things that she do in her life. She thinks that she is influenced the most when she becomes a leader to a small thing or maybe an activity because she don’t usually like talking to people. And becoming a leader is a away for her to actually speak her mind and say all the things that she want to say that may influence some people or her group mates. She is also close to everyone in her family. She graduated grade school at Alinggan-Banaban Elementary School and she attended High School at Tococ National High School. Ma. Jasmin don’t have a favorite subject, it depends to her mind and how she would handle a certain activity from a subject. She is a student who is care free but really likes to overthink a lot even if herself and her grade are actually doing fine. One of her ambitions in life is to become a flight attendant. It has been a long dream of her to become one and is hoping that she will actually achieve it in the near future. One of her memorable experience is when she was 16, and joined her first real pageant. She learned a lot of things there, and that was an experience she will never forget in her life.
