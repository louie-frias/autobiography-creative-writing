Broken Family

by Jonalyn Cervas

Under the shadow of broken bonds, a story of muffled cries is unfolded.

Love stops in broken houses, echoing broken remnants of what once was.

There were muted cries in empty hallways, laughter, and now quiet.

Damage spun in broken hearts, a family ripped apart, an intricate structure unraveled.

Memories fade, pieces stay, the result of long-lasting pain. Reflections fade in broken mirrors,

the silent enemy of a broken family.

Still, resiliency runs through the ks, a strength hidden, a grieving heart.

There are quiet screams in empty hallways where there used to be dancing laughter.

A family shattered, a web unraveled, the harm weaved in broken hearts.

Memories fade, fragments endure,

A patchwork of chronic agony. A broken family's quiet enemy, reflections dim in broken mirrors.

However, resiliency, unspoken strength, and a grieving heart are woven across the gaps.

New stories emerge from shattered roots, and optimism finds space in the past.
