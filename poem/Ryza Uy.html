Friendship: A Guiding Light in Life's Journey

by Ryza Uy

Friendship is a light that illuminates the way,

Through life's darkest storms, guiding us astray.

It's a shelter in times of turmoil and strife,

A constant presence, adding color to our life.

Friendship isn't measured by likes or popularity,

But by the love and support, shared with sincerity.

It's a shoulder to lean on when tears start to flow,

A listening ear, when our hearts overflow.

Friendship is laughter ringing, like a joyful melody,

Creating memories, painting life with hues so vividly.

It's the comfort of silence, a bond unspoken,

Understanding without words, a language unbroken.

Friendship is a secret keeper, guarding our fears,

Trusting and confiding, wiping away our tears.

It's a hand to hold in times of doubt or despair,

A reminder that we are never truly alone, always cared.

Friendship is a garden, where trust and love bloom,

Nurtured by time, it transcends any room.

It's the essence of loyalty, unwavering and true,

A safe haven where hearts find solace when blue.

So, let us celebrate this gift, so precious and rare,

For true friendship is beyond any Google search or snare.

It's a bond that enriches, giving life a higher worth,

A treasure that cannot be found in the vast digital surf.
